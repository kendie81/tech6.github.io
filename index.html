Unit Overview

What You'll Learn & Make

This six-week unit takes you from complete beginner to confident maker. You'll learn the fundamentals of sewing — safety, machine use, fabric knowledge, and hand skills — while working toward two finished projects you get to take home.

Along the way, you'll explore how digital tools like Canva and Cricut connect to sewing, and discover the wide range of careers in fashion, textiles, and design.

🧵
Machine Skills — Thread a sewing machine, wind a bobbin, sew straight seams and backstitch
🪡
Hand Sewing — Sew on a button with a proper shank, knot and fasten off neatly
✂️
Cutting & Measuring — Measure fabric, mark with chalk, and cut accurately and safely
🌡️
Ironing & Laundry — Press seams, identify care symbols, sort and wash fabrics correctly
🎨
Digital Design — Create an SVG in Canva and understand how Cricut machines use design files
💼
Careers — Explore 12+ careers across fashion design, technical textiles, and creative specialties

Essential Questions

Big Ideas to Think About

How do I choose the right materials? What makes one fabric better than another for a given project?
