Growing Food:
Seeds to Harvest

A 6-week unit exploring techniques used to grow food — from seed saving to harvest, with a focus on Canadian agriculture and sustainability.

6 Weeks 1 × 50 min / week Grow Tower & Raised Beds SGC · CTPS · SASM
Unit overview
Grade level
Grade 6–7 (ages 11–13)
Duration
6 weeks · 1 class/week · 50 min
Setting
Classroom + grow tower + raised beds
Ongoing roles
Waterer, Observer, Recorder, Tower Lead
Achievement indicators
🌱Save vegetable seeds from mature, harvested vegetables
🫘Start vegetable seeds
💧Water and fertilize plants
Global competencies
SGC
Sustainable Green Communities
Students connect food-growing to sustainability, water conservation, and community food systems.
CTPS
Critical Thinking & Problem Solving
Students compare growing methods, diagnose plant problems, and draw evidence-based conclusions.
SASM
Self-Awareness & Self-Management
Students manage care responsibilities, reflect on contributions, and self-assess with a checklist.
