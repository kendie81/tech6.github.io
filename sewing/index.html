Your Projects
Three Things You'll Make
🔖
Project 1 — Week 2
Fabric Bookmark with Button
Your first machine-sewn project. You'll thread the machine, sew straight seams, clip corners, turn and press your bookmark, then hand-sew a button with a proper shank.
20% of your grade
→
🎒
Project 2 — Weeks 3–5
Cinch Sac Drawstring Book Bag
A real, functional bag you design, measure, cut, and sew yourself. You'll choose your fabric, build the bag body, attach a drawstring casing, and thread the cord so it opens and closes.
40% of your grade
→
🎨
Project 3 — Week 6
Canva SVG Design File
Create a bold, original graphic in Canva that could be cut on a Cricut machine. You'll learn the difference between SVGs and regular images, and between sublimation and heat-transfer vinyl.
20% of your grade
→

Learning Outcomes
What You'll Know & Be Able to Do
🧵
Machine Skills — Thread a sewing machine, wind a bobbin, sew straight seams and backstitch to lock them
🪡
Hand Sewing — Sew on a button with a proper shank and knot off neatly at the back
✂️
Cutting & Measuring — Measure fabric accurately, mark with chalk, and cut safely using fabric scissors
🌡️
Ironing & Laundry — Press seams, read care label symbols, and sort and wash fabrics correctly
🎨
Digital Design — Create an SVG in Canva and understand how Cricut machines use design files
💼
Careers — Name and describe 12+ careers across fashion design, technical textiles, and creative specialties
